Frequently Asked Questions

What warranty comes with your products?

All our products come with a minimum one-year warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase.

Can I upgrade components of my computer after purchase?

Yes, most components can be upgraded. We recommend contacting our support team for compatibility advice before upgrading.

Do you offer technical support for software issues?

Yes, we provide technical support for software issues. Please reach out to our helpdesk with your query.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email that you can use to track your order on the courier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products, provided they are returned in their original condition.

Do you offer international shipping?

At the moment we support shipping only in Romania. Shipping costs and times may vary depending on the destination.

How can I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or change your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How secure is shopping on NV-Shop? Is my data protected?

Your security is our priority. We use SSL encryption to protect your data and ensure safe online transactions.

Can I get a discount on bulk orders?

Yes, discounts on bulk orders are available. Please contact our sales team for more information.
